Code Of Conduct

  • Participants shall accept full responsibility for their own work and ensure to work as a team.
  • Be Respectful. Harrasment of any kind will not be tolerable.
  • No participant shall engage in any malicious/illegal activities which do not adhere to general code of conduct for developers. Participants will be immediately disqualified If they are found indulging in malpractice.
  • Attendees should not use sexualised images, activities, or other material both in their hacks and during the event.

Rules of the Hackathon

  • Maximum team size is 2 members. We would suggest 1 designer and 1 programmer or both programmers.
  • Single participants can also register. We will find teams for you to join on spot.
  • Participants should bring their own laptops. Tables and internet connection will be provided.
  • Participants can build anything(software/hardware) but should not deviate from the theme.
  • Participants are not allowed to exit the hackathon hall during the duration of hackathon.
  • Food and Refreshments will be provided by us.
  • You are allowed to use any Open Source Softwares or libraries/frameworks.
  • Your final product will be evaluated by our mentors and the decision of mentors is final.
