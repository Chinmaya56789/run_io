About Run IO

We believe that the future of the world holds a lot of uncertainties. We understand that it is our responsibility to work on the issues that are evident in the near or far future. Run IO Hackathon is all about bringing together ideas for the long RUN and developing products/services to enhance the future. It's a first of its kind event in Eastern India in which we try to change the shape of the future in 24 hours. Taking place in the beautiful city of Rourkela, at the National Institute of Technology which is one of the most prestigious technical institutions of the country, we manage to bring together a diverse set of talents from a vast talent pool in the institution to organize this hackathon.
Moreover, the hackathon takes place along with the largest tech fest of Eastern India (INNOVISION), with an attendance of 1500+ students from all over the country.
We welcome programmers from all around the world to collaborate with us for the Hackathon accompanied with guest lectures and developer talks. So let your imagination go wild and build something worth marvelling. Collaborate, create and help humanity make the next giant leap. Hackathons can be tough, but we'll ensure your survival!
