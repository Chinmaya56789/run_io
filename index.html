About Run I/O

NIT Rourkela is one of the most prestigious technical institutions of the country, and rightfully so. With a diverse set of talent pool in the institute itself we welcome programmers from all around the world to collaborate with us for a 12 hour Hackathon accompanied with guest lectures and developer talks. So let your imagination go wild and build something worth marvelling. Collaborate, create and help humanity make the next giant leap. Hackathons can be tough, but we'll ensure your survival!

And much more!

Theme

The Hackathon theme is "the future" and participants are allowed to make a web based, app based or a robotic project with a vision to solve a problem that you think is evident in the future of the world.

Rules

  1. Maximum team size is 3 members. We would suggest 1 designer and 2 programmers.
  2. Single participants can also register. We will find teams for you to join on spot.
  3. Participants should bring their own laptops. Tables and internet connection will be provided.
  4. Participants can build anything(software/hardware) but should not deviate from the theme.
  5. Participants are not allowed to exit the hackathon hall during the duration of hackathon.
  6. Food and Refreshments will be provided by us.
  7. You are allowed to use any Open Source Softwares or libraries/frameworks.
  8. Your final product will be evaluated by our mentors and the decision of mentors is final.

Code Of Conduct

  1. Participants shall accept full responsibility for their own work and ensure to work as a team.
  2. Be Respectful. Harrasment of any kind will not be tolerable.
  3. No participant shall engage in any malicious/illegal activities which do not adhere to general code of conduct for developers. Participants will be immediately disqualified If they are found indulging in malpractice.
  4. attendees should not use sexualised images, activities, or other material both in their hacks and during the event.
